About Us

Beaver Rider is a web application that focus on provide safe ride share to OSU student. Our team is made of all OSU students, and we believe Beaver Rider can not only provide a chance to get a safe ride and some extra money, but also a chance to meet people and make friedns.

How to use

Beaver Rider is a web application for OSU students. For using the service of Beaver Rider, you need to log in first. Click the log in button first and log in with onid username and password.
